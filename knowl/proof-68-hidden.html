The implications \(\Rightarrow\) are clear. To show \(3) \Rightarrow 1)\text{,}\) we show the contrapositive. If \(m\neq 0\text{,}\) consider \(Rm \subseteq M\text{.}\) By [[Mathematics/Commutative Algebra/Results/Lemma - Nonzero Element Localizes|Lemma]], there is a maximal ideal in \(\Supp(Rm)\text{,}\) and by [[Mathematics/Commutative Algebra/Results/Lemma - SES Support|Lemma]] applied to the inclusion \(Rm \subseteq M\text{,}\) this maximal ideal is in \(\Supp(M)\) as well.
