Lemma 6.17. Nonzero Element Localizes.

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module, and \(m\in M\text{.}\) The following are equivalent: (a) \(m=0\) in \(M\text{.}\) (b) \(\frac{m}{1}=0\) in \(M_P\) for all \(P \in \Spec(R)\)[^1]. (c) \(\frac{m}{1}=0\) in \(M_P\) for all \(P \in \mSpec(R)\text{.}\)
