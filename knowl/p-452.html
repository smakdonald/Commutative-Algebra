Given a submodule \(N\) of \(M\text{,}\) we can apply the [[Mathematics/Commutative Algebra/Results/Theorem - Localization is Exact|Theorem]] to the short exact sequence
\begin{equation*} \begin{CD} 0@>>>N@>>>M@>>>M/N@>>>0 \end{CD} \end{equation*}
and conclude that that \(W^{-1}(M/N) \cong W^{-1}M / W^{-1}N\text{.}\)
