Example 5.18. Field of Fractions is a Localization.

Note that if \(R\) is a domain, the equivalence relation simplifies to \(rw'=r'w\text{,}\) so \(R \subseteq W^{-1}R \subseteq \mathrm{Frac}(R)\)[^1], and in particular \(W^{-1}R\) is a domain too. In particular, \(\mathrm{Frac}(R)\) is a localization of \(R\text{.}\)
