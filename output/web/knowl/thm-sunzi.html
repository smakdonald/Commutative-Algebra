Theorem A.12. Sunzi's Remainder Theorem.

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(I_1, \ldots, I_n\) be pairwise coprime ideals in \(R\text{,}\) meaning \(I_i + I_j = R\) for all \(i \neq j\text{.}\) Then \(I := I_1 \cap \cdots \cap I_n = I_1 \cdots I_n\text{,}\) and there is an isomorphism of rings
\begin{equation*} \begin{CD} R/I@>\cong>> R/I_1 \times \cdots \times R/I_n\\\\ r + I@>>>(r+I_1, \ldots, r+I_n) \end{CD} \end{equation*}
