In summary, any short exact sequence encodes an inclusion and its cokernel, or equivalently a surjection an its kernel. To give a short exact sequence
\begin{equation*} \begin{CD} 0@>>>A@>>>B@>>>C@>>>0 \end{CD} \end{equation*}
is the same as giving an inclusion of modules \(A \subseteq B\) and the corresponding quotient module \(B/A\text{.}\)
