Let \(A\subseteq B \subseteq C\) be rings. It follows from the definitions that
\begin{equation*} \text{(a)}\quad\quad\quad \begin{align*} &A\subseteq B \,\,\text{module-finite}\\ &\quad \quad \quad \quad \text{and}\\ &B\subseteq C \,\,\text{module-finite} \end{align*} \Longrightarrow \hspace{2em} A\subseteq C \,\,\text{module-finite} \end{equation*}
