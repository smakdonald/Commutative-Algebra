Definition 4.11. Zariski Topology.

Let \(k\) be a field. The collection of subvarieties \(X \subseteq \A^n_k\) is the collection of closed subsets in a topology on \(\A^n_k\text{.}\) This is called the on \(\A^n_k\text{.}\) Any subvariety of \(\A^n_k\) obtains a Zariski topology as the subspace topology from \(\A^n_k\text{.}\)
