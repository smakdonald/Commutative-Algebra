Corollary 8.15. Dim \(<\)= EmbDim.

Let \((R,\fm,k)\) be a noetherian local ring. Then
\begin{equation*} \dim(R)=\min\{ n \ | \ \sqrt{(f_1,\dots,f_n)}=\fm \textrm{ for some } f_1,\dots,f_n \} \leqslant \mu(\fm). \end{equation*}
In particular, a noetherian local ring has finite dimension.
