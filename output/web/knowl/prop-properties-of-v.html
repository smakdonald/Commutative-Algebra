Proposition 3.13. Properties of V.

Let \(R\) be a ring, and \(I_{\lambda},J\) be ideals, not necessarily proper. (a) \(V(R) = \varnothing\) and \(V(0) = \Spec(R)\)[^1]. (b) If \(I \subseteq J\text{,}\) then \(V(J)\subseteq V(I)\text{.}\) (c) \(V(I) \cup V(J) = V(I \cap J)=V(IJ)\text{.}\) (d) \(\bigcap_{\lambda} V(I_{\lambda}) =V(\sum_{\lambda} I_{\lambda})\text{.}\)

Proof.
