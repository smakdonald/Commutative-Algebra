Corollary 7.55. F.G. Algebra Catenary Domain.

Let \(R\) be a finitely generated algebra or a quotient of a power series ring over a field. (a) \(R\) is catenary. If additionally \(R\) is a domain, then (b) \(R\) is equidimensional, and (c) \(\ht(I)=\dim(R)-\dim(R/I)\) for all ideals \(I\text{.}\)[^1]
