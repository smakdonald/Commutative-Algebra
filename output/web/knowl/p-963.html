A subset \(R \subseteq S\) of a ring \(S\) is a subring if \(R\) is also a ring with the structure induced by \(S\text{,}\) meaning that the each operation on \(R\) is the restrictions of the corresponding operation on \(S\) to \(R\text{,}\) and the \(0\) and \(1\) in \(R\) are the \(0\) and \(1\) in \(S\text{,}\) respectively.
