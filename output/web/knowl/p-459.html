By [[Mathematics/Commutative Algebra/Results/Proposition - NAK 1|NAK1]], there exists an element \(r\in 1+\fm\) that annihilates \(M\)[^1]. Notice that \(1 \notin \fm\text{,}\) so any such \(r\) must be outside of \(\fm\text{,}\) and thus a unit. Multiplying by its inverse, we conclude that \(1\) annihilates \(M\text{,}\) or equivalently, that \(M=0\text{.}\)
