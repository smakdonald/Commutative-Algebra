Example 6.24. Associated Primes of \((x^{2})\) in \(k[[x]]\).

Let \(k\) be a field and \(R = k[[x]]\)[^1]. Consider the ideal \(I = (x^2)\text{,}\) and the \(R\)-module \(M = R/I\text{.}\) If \(P\) is a prime ideal in \(R\) and \(P\) is associated to \(M\text{,}\) then \(P = \ann(r+I)\)[^2] for some \(r \in R\text{.}\)
