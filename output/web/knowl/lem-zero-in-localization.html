Lemma 5.36. Zero in Localization.

Let \(M\) be an \(R\)-module, and \(W\) a multiplicative set. Then the following are equivalent: - \(\frac{m}{w}\in W^{-1}M \ \text{ is zero}\) - \(vm=0 \textrm{ for some } v\in W\) - \(\ann_R(m) \cap W \neq \es.\) Note in particular that this holds for \(w=1\text{.}\)

Proof.
