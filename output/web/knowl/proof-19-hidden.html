We will do induction on \(n\text{.}\) The case \(n=1\) is a tautology. For \(n>1\text{,}\) consider the short exact sequence
\begin{equation*} \begin{CD} 0@>>>M^{n-1}@>>>M^{n}@>g>>M@>>>0 \end{CD} \end{equation*}
[provisional cross-reference: cite] [[Mathematics/Commutative Algebra/Results/Lemma - Noetherianity in Exact Sequences|Lemma]] and the inductive hypothesis give the desired conclusion.
