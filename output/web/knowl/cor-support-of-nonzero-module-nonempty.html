Corollary 6.18. Support of Nonzero Module Nonempty.

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module, and \(m\in M\text{.}\) The following are equivalent: (a) \(M=0\text{.}\) (b) \(M_P=0\) in \(M_P\) for all \(P \in \Spec(R)\)[^1]. (c) \(M_P=0\) in \(M_P\) for all \(P \in \mSpec(R)\text{.}\)[^2]
