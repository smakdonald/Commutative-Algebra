Proposition 1.47. F.G.-by-Intl-Modfin.

Consider a ring extension \(A\subseteq R\text{.}\) (a) If \(r \in R\) is integral over \(A\text{,}\) then \(A[r]\) is module-finite over \(A\text{.}\) (b) If \(r_1,\dots,r_t \in R\) are integral over \(A\text{,}\) then \(A[r_1,\dots,r_t]\) is module-finite over \(A\text{.}\)
